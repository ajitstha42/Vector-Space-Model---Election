The stories that matter on money and politics in the race for the White House

FT journalists gathered for an exclusive subscriber webinar on September 12 to assess who is more likely to prevail in the race for the White House after the highly anticipated and only debate between Donald Trump and Kamala Harris on September 10. 

Our expert panel was moderated by Peter Spiegel, US managing editor, and included Lauren Fedor, US political correspondent, Rana Foroohar, global business columnist, and Gideon Rachman, chief foreign affairs commentator.

It analysed the debate performances, key policy positions, and the latest polling data to provide insights into which candidate had gained the upper hand. We explored how the candidates are addressing the economy, business healthcare, and foreign policy, and what their respective strategies reveal about their path to victory.
